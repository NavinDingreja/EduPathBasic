Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) / Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)
About: 4-year degree in various branches like Computer, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, etc.
Career Options: Software Engineer, Mechanical Engineer, Civil Engineer, Data Scientist.
🩺
Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS)
About: 5.5-year professional course to become a doctor.
Career Options: Doctor, Surgeon, Medical Officer, Researcher.
💊
Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm)
About: 4-year degree focusing on drug formulation, chemistry, and pharmacology.
Career Options: Pharmacist, Drug Inspector, Medical Representative, Research Associate.
🔬
Bachelor of Science in Biotechnology (B.Sc Biotechnology)
About: Studies genetics, cell biology, and bioprocesses used in healthcare and agriculture.
Career Options: Biotechnologist, Lab Technician, Research Scientist, Quality Analyst.
💻
Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
About: 3-year course in computer programming, software, and IT systems.
Career Options: Software Developer, Web Developer, Data Analyst, System Administrator.
✈️
Bachelor of Science in Aviation
About: Covers principles of flight, air traffic control, and airport operations.
Career Options: Pilot, Air Traffic Controller, Aviation Manager, Flight Dispatcher.
🧪
Bachelor of Science in Chemistry / Physics / Mathematics
About: Pure science courses focusing on theoretical and practical aspects.
Career Options: Scientist, Research Assistant, Lecturer, Lab Technician.
🧫
Bachelor of Science in Microbiology
About: Studies microorganisms and their impact on humans and the environment.
Career Options: Microbiologist, Pathologist, Food Safety Officer, Research Assistant.
🧠
Bachelor of Psychology (B.Sc Psychology)
About: Studies human behavior, mental health, and cognitive processes.
Career Options: Psychologist, Counselor, HR Specialist, Clinical Researcher.
🌌
Bachelor of Science in Environmental Science
About: Focuses on ecology, pollution control, and sustainability.
Career Options: Environmental Consultant, Wildlife Conservationist, Research Officer.
